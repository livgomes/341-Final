Monatana is the 4th largest state in America, located in the Western Region. It is economy is mainly relantant on agriculture and ranching, due to the vast landscape. It has a population of 1 million and is well known for its nickname "big sky country" and "The last best place". Additional to farming and ranching Montana is known for its recreational activities such as hiking, hunting, fly fishing, biking and so much more. This webpage will focus primarly on the small university town called Missoula, located in western Montana.
