Liv's Bio

My Olivia Gomes is a 25 year old student, With a Major in Environemental Science and Minor in Media Arts at the university of montanna. Originally from the small islands of Trinidad and Tobago, located within the Caribbean, Olivia has always found her self wanting to explore more of what this big world has to offer. With a job in learning and the will to explore, Olivia found a way to combine these two attributes, studying in colleges across the Globe. Currently Olivia has managed to explore through the schooling system; Costa Rica through attendence at The United World College of Costa Rica, Montana USA at The University of Montan and Tasmania AUS at The University of Tasmania. Through these experiences Olivia has met people from all cultures and backgrounds and had experiences of a Lifetime. This personal webpage is a place for her to share these experiences and keep a live diary to some of the best and most memerable times of her life.
